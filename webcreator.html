Welcome to the Digital Ninjas Website Request!
To request your website, please follow these steps on the form:

1. Enter Your Email: Provide us with your email address.

2. Type of Website: Select the type of website you need.

3. Branding: Share your branding requirements.

4. Text on Website: Provide the text content for your website.

5. Style: Describe the desired style or theme.

6. Subpages: Outline the specific pages or sections you want.

7. Additional Information: Include any extra details or requests.

Once you're ready, submit the form. Our team will review your request and reach out to discuss further details, provide a proposal, and answer any questions. Thank you for choosing Digital Ninjas Website Request! We look forward to creating your website.
